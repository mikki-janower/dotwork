Reflections on Practice

Reflections on Practice documents the creative process in progress. The series of booklets chronicles six months of lessons across disciplines, spanning print design, web development, drone photography, and microfiction.

Each book combines traditional modes of process documentation with found essays and written ruminations. The result is an intimate exploration of academic learning and personal growth.

Compiled and designed over the course of the Fall 2019 semester at the Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts
