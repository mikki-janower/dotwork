Mikki Janower is a graphic designer and art director based in Brooklyn. Her work reflects her multidisciplinary background, robust visual research, and acute typophilia. She loves freelance projects and collaborations, particularly work skewed towards music and the arts.

She recently recieved a BFA in Communication Design and Writing from Washington University in St. Louis and is currently looking for postgraduate opportunities. Previous clients include Nike and The Governor's Ball Music Festival; previous internships include The Collected Works.
