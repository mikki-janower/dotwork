The Creative Independent

The Creative Independent is a growing resource of emotional and practical guidance for creative people. Over a two-year period, I helped to steward the site's design, a constant and adaptive process of assessing and expanding the spectacular original design by Laurel Schwulst. I also led design on special projects, like zines and microsites; and interviewed some artists I admire.

Original site design and direction: Laurel Schwulst
Site development: Elliott Cost
Creative direction: Amanda Rios and Brandon Stosuy
