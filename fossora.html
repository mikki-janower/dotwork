Björk: fossora

Immersive web design surrounding the release of fossora, the tenth studio album by Björk.

A follow-up to Utopia (2017), fossora explores a fungal wonderland rich with mycelial networks, human entanglements, and thunderous basslines. fossora.com builds an environment around the album, siting video stills, behind-the-scenes content, and written commentary within a lush, dark landscape.

Currently in its beginning stages, the site will roll out in phases over the coming month. Built in community with Björk, OLI Records, M/M Paris, and the creative team at Squarespace.

Album artwork and CGI: M/M Paris
Creative Director: Satu Pelkonen
Creative Consultant: Albert Chang
Producer: Sion Prys
Developer: Siman Li
Made with Squarespace
