fossora
Björk

Immersive virtual environment for fossora, the critically acclaimed and Grammy-nominated studio album by Björk. A follow-up to Utopia (2017), fossora explores a fungal wonderland rich with mycelial networks, human entanglements, and thunderous basslines. In digital space, the album unfolds into a lush, dark landscape, siting excerpts; marginalia; and written commentary from Björk.

The site launched in stages — in step with the eponymous album's content — over several months. Built between NYC and Reykjavík in community with Björk, OLI Records, M/M Paris, and the creative team at Squarespace. Björk's team will continue to build out the site over the coming months.

Album artwork and CGI: M/M Paris
Creative Directors: Satu Pelkonen, Ben Hughes
Producers: Sion Prys, Erica Kung
Creative Consultant: Albert Chang
Developer: Siman Li
