Sober 212021

Sober 21 is a compendium of essays by, and interviews with, sober musicians. Through open conversation and intimate anecdotes, the zine raises awareness and provides resources for sobriety in the music industry.

The zine uses negative space in lieu of traditional forms of image-making, creating moments of noise, interruption, reflection, and pause.

Edited by Elia Einhorn and published by the Creative Independent.
