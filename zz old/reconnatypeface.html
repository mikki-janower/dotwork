Reconna Typeface

Reconna is a prototype inspired letterforms by found in 20th-century Italian print archives. The condensed serif has high ascenders and angled serifs. Glyph set includes numerals, basic punctuation, and a variety of symbols.

This typeface would not exist without instruction and encouragement from Alessio D'Ellena. You can download the full specimen here.
