Venn Diagramm

Venn Diagramm is an small-run art and publishing practice. Founded in collaboration with Maddy Angstreich, Venn Diagramm is our space for experimentation and play.

Publications are open-access online and hardcopies are priced along a sliding scale at markets and fairs. Venn Diagramm has shown work at Press Play, among other events; and is currently stocked in bookstores in New York and London Follow Venn Diagramm or visit www.venndiagramm.space for updates.
