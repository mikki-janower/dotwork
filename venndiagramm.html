Venn Diagramm

Venn Diagramm is an small-run art and publishing practice. Founded in collaboration with Maddy Angstreich, Venn Diagramm is our space for experimentation and play: works created through Venn Diagramm often distill our independent research.

Select works are free to print, assemble, and share, or available for purchase at events, bookstores, and shops. Follow Venn Diagramm or visit www.venndiagramm.space for updates.
