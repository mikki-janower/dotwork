Sampler Typeface

Sampler Display & Sampler Small are proportional cross-stitch sisters. Built from the same stitch-inspired module, the two fonts are designed to be used at a 3:1 ratio. When used this way, they adhere to the same grid, echoing the behavior of a real-world cross stitch. Their glyphs are modeled after a lettering style popular with 19th-century stitchers, commonly seen on samplers from that time.

The Sampler mini-family is open-access. If you'd like to use it, feel free to reach out.
